Hosts Review

There are a number of different parameters to look at when it comes to web hosting providers and so to narrow down the search for the best one, we are going to be evaluating from the perspective of an individual with an anticipated low traffic website perhaps expanding in the future.

Amazon AWS

  • 3 month free trials
  • Scales up well
  • Huge infrastructure
  • 750 GB memory in lightsail package

Negatives

The website is confusing to navigate and unclear about terms.

godaddy

  • 6 dollar per month economy plan
  • 100 GB storage
  • Easily scalable

Negatives

Perhaps not as easily scalable as Amazon.

bluehost

  • Free domain for 1 year
  • 3 dollar per month cheapest plan
  • The cheapest economy plan
  • 50 GB SSD storage

Negatives

Unclear about how terms change when scaling up.

The best website

Godaddy is the best website for our purposes. While bluehost would probably be the easiest and cheapest to get started, Godaddy represents a good mix of cheap start-up costs and simplistic scalability.
