Valentines Day

Date: February 14th

Origins

Valentines day was established in 496 by Pope Gelasius in honor of the martyr St. Valentine. There is some debate as to whether the pagan mating tradition of Lupercalia has leaked into the tradition of Valentines day as it was celebrated at a similar time.
