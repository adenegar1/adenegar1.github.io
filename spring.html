Spring Holidays: Easter, St Patricks Day, and Cinco De Mayo

Start: March 20th
End: June 21st

Easter

Easter is a day commemorating Jesus' resurection from the dead. Today we celebrate Easter by allowing the Easter bunny to steal all of our eggs and hide them.

When is Easter?

The first Sunday after the full Moon that occurs on or after the spring equinox.

St. Patricks Day

When is St. Patricks Day?

March 17th

Why do we celebrate St. Patricks day?

The day commemorates Saint Patrick and the arrival of Christianity in Ireland, and celebrates the heritage and culture of the Irish in general.

Cinco De Mayo

Cinco de Mayo means fifth of May in english and it celebrates Mexico's 1862 victory in the Battle of Puebla. Contrary to popular belief, Cinco De Mayo is not Mexico's independence day.
